Introduction

Human is a kind of complicated and interesting animal. we eat food because we hungry,

"human" is our intelligence and personality. We all make choice everyday, your environment, carrer, lover...
are all the results of your choice. But, stand behind the mirror and ask yourself.
Are you really happy for all your choices? Does all your choices follow your heart? Do you really want to do your job for next 50 years?
